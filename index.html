   The Drake equation is a probabilistic argument used to estimate the number of active, communicative extraterrestrial civilizations in the Milky Way galaxy. Or more simply put, the odds of finding intelligent life in the universe. This experience is designed to educate and visualize this principle in a interactive and exiting way
